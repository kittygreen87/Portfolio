Visit Alta

Alta, Wyoming is nestled in the Teton Valley. It is an easy 45 minute drive to Jackson Hole, WY where you can enjoy the exciting restaurants, shopping, live music, and yearly events. Alta is also located near multiple ski destinations such as King Resort, Targhee Mountain, and Teton Village. During the summer months, come enjoy the hot air balloon festival, hike the Grand Tetons, or visit Yellowstone National Park. There is something for everyone!
